About Me

Hey, I’m Dibyanshu, a systems developer, hardware tinkerer, and retro computing enthusiast. I build things with low-level systems, high-level graphics pipelines, and occasionally solder together custom handheld consoles.

This blog, powered by the Tectonic theme, is my digital workshop. It is where I document my technical explorations, architectural diagrams, systems research, and homebrew projects.

Core Philosophy

I believe in clean, readable, mono-spaced typography and simple, bold UI elements. I appreciate functional and tactile systems—from low-power mechanical switches to robust retro-brutalist digital environments.

Technical Timeline & History

  • 2023 - Present: Systems Architect at NeoRetro Labs. Designing low-latency WebSocket infrastructure and real-time networking protocols for browser-based games.
  • 2020 - 2023: Hardware/Software Integration Engineer at CoreBound Technologies. Slashed CPU/Memory footprints on embedded platforms and optimized canvas vs. WebGL pipelines.
  • 2017 - 2020: Independent Systems & Emulation Researcher. Contributed to retro sprite-rendering architectures and homebrew software ecosystems.
